Welcome to the ASF Self-serve Portal. We offer a wide range of tools for projects, committers, and users of our software and communities, allowing you to request new resources or change and configure existing ones without requiring manual interaction with the infrastructure team. You'll find our current self-serve offerings in the three panels below, sorted by the access level you will need in order to make a request. If you do not have the required access level, we advise that you contact someone in your project with the appropriate access and ask for their help. The list of features is continuously expanding, and as more self-serve offers become available, they will be listed on this landing page, as well as in the navigation bar at the top of this page.

Services available to all project committees

These features are available to anyone currently serving on a Project Management Committee (PMC) or Podling Project Management Committee (PPMC). Foundation members also have access to these tools. You will be required to authenticate via our OAuth service before you can submit requests.

  • Create a Jira project
  • Create a Git repository
  • Create a mailing list
  • Manage mailing lists
  • Create a Confluence wiki space
  • Archive a Confluence wiki space

Services available to all committers of any ASF project

These features are available to anyone with a committer account at the foundation, regardless of which project you are affiliated with.

  • Edit your ASF identity
  • Reset your password
  • OTP calculator

Services available to all contributors or users

These features are available to anyone on the Internet. You do not need to be a committer on any project to make use of them. All you need is a valid, working email address for verification purposes.

  • Create a Jira account


Don't see what you need?

Feel free to check out our introductory and detailed online documentation.
